The Locations of
Red Dead Redemption 2

Ambarino

The snowy and mountainous lands of Ambarino are both desolate yet a wonderous site to behold. Despite this, traveling there may prove challenging. Wildlife such as wolves and bears roam the area, and there are the dangers of the chilling temperatures. However, it may well be worth it, as there is a natural beauty to be found in all of this snow.

Lemoyne

Do you want something more modern? Then Lemoyne is the right place for you, hosting the bustling city of Saint Denis where everything is modernized. Just be careful of the swamps and the alligators you'll find there.

New Hanover

Despite being the 2nd largest state, this area is mostly barren. The Heartlands take up a majority of this area, and it's where bison may roam in large open fields. However, it's not devoid of human life. Many towns, such as Valentine and Annesburg reside here. You may also camp in the many forests encompasing the area.
